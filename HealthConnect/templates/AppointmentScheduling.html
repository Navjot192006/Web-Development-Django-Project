Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance can I book an appointment?

Our scheduling system shows availability up to 3 months in advance for most providers. Some specialists may have different booking windows depending on demand.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a fee, depending on the provider's policy.

Can I reschedule my appointment online?

Yes! You can reschedule any appointment through our patient portal up to 24 hours before your scheduled time. Just log in to your account and select "Reschedule" next to your upcoming appointment.

What information do I need to book an appointment?

For new patients, we'll need your full name, date of birth, contact information, and insurance details (if applicable). For follow-up visits, just your name and the reason for your visit are typically sufficient.

How do virtual consultations work?

Virtual consultations are conducted through our secure video platform. After booking, you'll receive a link to join the video call at your scheduled time. You'll need a device with a camera, microphone, and internet connection.
