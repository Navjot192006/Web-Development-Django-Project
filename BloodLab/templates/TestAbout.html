About BloodLab Diagnostics

Our Story

Founded in 2005, BloodLab Diagnostics began as a small laboratory with a big vision: to revolutionize diagnostic testing through accuracy, speed, and patient-centered care. What started as a team of five passionate professionals has grown into one of the region's most trusted diagnostic laboratories, serving thousands of patients and healthcare providers each year.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation. We were among the first in our region to implement fully automated testing systems, reducing human error and improving turnaround times. In 2015, we opened our second location, and by 2020, we had expanded to five state-of-the-art facilities across the state.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and affordable diagnostic testing that empowers patients and healthcare providers to make informed decisions about health and wellness. We are committed to excellence in every test we perform and every interaction we have.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in diagnostic testing, recognized for our technological leadership, clinical expertise, and uncompromising commitment to patient care. We aim to expand access to advanced diagnostics while maintaining the personal touch that defines our service.

At BloodLab Diagnostics, we believe that accurate diagnostics are the foundation of effective healthcare. Our team of board-certified pathologists, PhD scientists, and certified medical technologists work together to ensure that every test meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.
